Welcome to Baldwin Nurseries

Celebrating 30 years growing for you in 2025

We're a family owned and operated nursery specializing in trees, shrubs and perennials. We have numerous collections such as native shrubs, trees and perennials, magnolias, Japanese maples, deer-resistant plants, and more. Our display gardens feature young specimens as well as more mature shrubs and trees, and we're always adding to the mix. With more than 5 acres, there is something for everyone.
