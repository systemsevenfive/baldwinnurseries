Our Guarantee

We guarantee our plants to be true to name and healthy when they leave our property, and for the current growing season only.

How you treat your new plants affects how well they do: after-care is especially important in their first season, both pre- and post-planting.

Adequate watering, mulching and correct planting technique will help ensure success with your new leafy acquisitions.

If you have any questions or concerns about your plant's care, please talk to us at the nursery or call or text ASAP. We are always happy to help with any advice on a solution to most problems!

Thank you again for your support over the years!
